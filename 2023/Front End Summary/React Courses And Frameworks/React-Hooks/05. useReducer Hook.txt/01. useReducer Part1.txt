(3) Different Between 'reduce' Js Method And 'useReducer' Hook
---------------------------------------------------------------

			reduce					   useReducer
	-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	array.reduce(reducer , initialValue)           useReducer(reducer , initialState)

	SingleValue = reducer(Acc , itemValue)	       newState = reducer(currentState , Action)

	Returns A Single Value 			       Returns Pair Of Values (newState , Dispatch)
