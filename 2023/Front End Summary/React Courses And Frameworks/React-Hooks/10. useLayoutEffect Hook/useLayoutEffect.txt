(1) Different Between 'useEffect' And 'useLayoutEffect'
------------------------------------------------------
==> useEffect : Make Re-Rendering After Any Change In The Component 
==> useLayoutEffect : The signature is identical to useEffect, but it fires synchronously after all DOM mutations
