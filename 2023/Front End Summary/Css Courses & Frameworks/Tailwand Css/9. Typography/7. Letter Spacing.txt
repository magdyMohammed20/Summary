(1) Letter Spacing
--------------------------
==> Utilities for controlling the tracking (letter spacing) of an element.

Class                 Properties
-------------------------------------------------------------
.tracking-tighter	    letter-spacing: -0.05em;
.tracking-tight	      letter-spacing: -0.025em;
.tracking-normal	    letter-spacing: 0;
.tracking-wide	      letter-spacing: 0.025em;
.tracking-wider	      letter-spacing: 0.05em;
.tracking-widest	    letter-spacing: 0.1em;


(2) Samples
-------------

  <p class="tracking-tighter font-serif">magdy mohammed</p>
  <p class="tracking-tight font-sans">magdy mohammed</p>
  <p class="tracking-normal font-serif">magdy mohammed</p>
  <p class="tracking-wide font-sans">magdy mohammed</p>
  <p class="tracking-wider font-serif">magdy mohammed</p>
  <p class="tracking-widest font-serif">magdy mohammed</p>


(3) Responsive Letter Spacing
-----------------------------------

  <p class="tracking-tight sm:tracking-normal md:tracking-wide lg:tracking-normal xl:tracking-tight"></p>
